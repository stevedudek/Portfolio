I make light-up art from small wearables to large installations for event spaces. My smaller works echo animal and themes that encapsulate lights in fresh, creative, and unexpected ways. “Did you make that yourself?” For larger event pieces, LEDs are organized in programmable spiky balls or two-dimensional screens. I emphasize portable, cost-effective, reusable, and light. I combine electronics with fabric for fun, tactile, illuminated pieces.
